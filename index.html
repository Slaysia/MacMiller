About Mac Miller

Miller’s slow, minimalist, spacey soundscapes have delivered baselines and bars for 5 consecutive albums starting with Blue Slide Park - the Pittsburgh rapper won’t be second guessed. While he admits he aims to make his best album with every release, his discography tends to explore new worlds and fresh concepts with each addition rather than a continually improving take on the same feeling. He quickly racked up an enormous following as a good-natured, preternaturally talented wordsmith. He also began producing music under his own alter ego, Larry Fisherman.
After passing away at the age of 26, he received enormous amounts of love from fellow artist/close friends like Chance The Rapper, J Cole, Wiz Khalifa, and Post Malone all over social media. Miller became a prolific collaborator, working with other rappers, jazz musicians and acclaimed producers. Making him a legend, a lover, and in inspiration.
